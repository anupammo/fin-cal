FAQs about TDS Calculators

Can I use online TDS calculators for all types of income?

Yes, online TDS calculators are versatile and can handle various income sources, including salary, interest, and more.

How does interest on late payment affect my overall tax liability?

Late payment of TDS incurs interest, impacting your financials. It's crucial to pay on time to avoid unnecessary penalties.

Are TDS rates uniform across all income brackets?

No, TDS rates vary based on the type of income and the amount. It's essential to be aware of the specific rates applicable to your income.

Can businesses claim a refund for excess TDS deducted?

Yes, businesses can claim a refund for excess TDS deducted by filing a return and providing necessary documentation.

Is TDS applicable to NRI income in India?

Yes, TDS is applicable to Non-Resident Indian (NRI) income in India, subject to specific rules and rates.

How can I check my TDS deductions online?

Most financial institutions provide an online portal where you can check your TDS deductions. Alternatively, you can use the government's e-filing website.
