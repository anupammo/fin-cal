Atal Pension Yojana

Atal Pension Yojana (APY) is a government-backed pension scheme aimed at providing a guaranteed minimum pension to the unorganized sector workers. It is a voluntary contribution-based scheme that allows individuals to save for their retirement and receive a regular income after they retire. An Atal Pension Yojana investment calculator is a tool that can help investors estimate the amount of money they can save under the scheme and plan their finances accordingly.

How Does a Atal Pension Yojana Investment Calculator Work?

An Atal Pension Yojana investment calculator is a simple tool that takes into account two main inputs: the age of the investor and the expected monthly pension amount. Based on these inputs, the calculator estimates the monthly contribution required to achieve the desired pension amount. To calculate the monthly contribution required for an Atal Pension Yojana account, the calculator uses a formula that takes into account the age of the investor, the expected monthly pension amount, and the investment period.

The maturity amount of a Atal Pension Yojana investment is calculated using the following formula:

P = (A * F) / [(1 + r)^(n/12) - 1
Where:
P = monthly contribution
A = expected monthly pension amount
F = accumulation factor based on the age of the investor
r = expected rate of return (in percentage)
n = investment period (in months)
