FAQ - Frequently asked questions

What is the interest rate offered on Post Office Monthly Income Scheme Account (MIS)?

An interest rate of 7.4% p.a. is applicable on Post Office Monthly Income Scheme Account (MIS) currently.

Is Post Office MIS suitable for senior citizens?

Yes, with Post Office MIS, senior citizens will be able to deposit their entire life savings into the account and earn interest to cover their monthly expenses.

Can we withdraw money from MIS before maturity?

Yes, the premature withdrawal facility is allowed after 1 year. However, if you withdraw before 3 years a deduction of 2% on deposit and after 3 years a deduction of 1% on deposit is applicable.
