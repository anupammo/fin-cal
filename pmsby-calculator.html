Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Suraksha Yojana

Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Suraksha Yojana (PMJSY) is a government-backed life insurance scheme that provides financial protection to the family of the policyholder in the event of their untimely death. The scheme is available to individuals between the ages of 18 and 70 and offers different levels of coverage at an affordable premium. An investment calculator for PMJSY can help investors estimate the premium amount and the sum assured under the scheme.

How Does a Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Suraksha Yojana Calculator Work?

A PMJSY investment calculator is a tool that takes into account two main inputs: the age of the investor and the sum assured. Based on these inputs, the calculator estimates the premium amount that the investor will need to pay to receive the chosen sum assured. To calculate the premium amount for a PMJSY policy, the calculator uses a formula that takes into account the age of the investor, the sum assured, and the mortality rate.

The maturity amount of a PMSBY investment is calculated using the following formula:

Premium = (Sum assured * Mortality rate) / 1000
Where:
Premium = the premium amount to be paid
Sum assured = the amount of life insurance coverage chosen by the investor
Mortality rate = the rate of death among the population of the investor's age group
